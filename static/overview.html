Scoring logic

Bull (0–6) — price above each SMA. Confirms bullish trend.

Dip (0–6) — price is below an SMA, or closer to it than usual vs recent history. Flags a potential buy-the-dip entry.

Total — Bull + Dip. Best setups: high bull and high dip (strong trend + pullback).

  • Strong BUY — bull ≥ 5 and dip ≥ 3
  • LONG · Dip — above all SMAs + dip forming
  • Strong LONG — above all SMAs
  • Dip BUY — bull ≥ 4 and dip ≥ 3
  • LONG — bull ≥ 5
  • Lean LONG — above at least half
  • Avoid LONG — above 0–1 SMAs
