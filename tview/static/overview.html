How scanning works

★ Pinned stocks always appear in the overview (manage on the Watchlist page).

Random large caps fill out the rest of today's scan (~55/day from a ~170-stock pool).

A watchlist entry needs all three pullback checks — uptrend intact, at the 100 SMA, and recently extended.

  • ✓ Trend OK — weekly & monthly above 200 SMA, or 5+/6 SMAs bullish
  • ✓ At 100 — daily price within ±3% of the 100 SMA
  • ✓ Was high — was ≥5% above daily 100 SMA within the last ~4 weeks

Pattern confirms

Extra daily-bar signals when a stock is at support. More ✓ = higher rank within the watchlist.

  • ✓ Hammer — long lower wick, small body (rejection of lows)
  • ✓ 3↑↓ — 3 up days, then 1–2 down into the SMA
  • ✓ Vol — down-day volume lighter than the up leg, or above-average on a hammer
